About NPSMUN

“You can imprison a man, but not an idea. You can exile a man, but not an idea. You can kill a man, but not an idea.” -Benazir Bhutto

Students at National Public School are all about exploring new ideas and over the last few years, NPS has been one among many to thrive in the explosive nature of the Model United Nations culture. It recognizes the ability of MUNs to provide an apt platform for expression and mold students’ personalities into brilliance.  With that, we are proud to present to you our first ever edition of NPSMUN, from 17 to 19 November, 2015. We strive to excel in what we pursue and are immensely excited to host the upcoming conference!
